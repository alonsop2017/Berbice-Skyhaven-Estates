Investment Opportunity Overview

Berbice Skyhaven Estates represents a premier, secure, and amenity-rich fly-in community tailored to the unique landscape and climate of coastal Guyana. This transformational 125-acre gated aviation community leverages the site's history as former rice paddies to create a resilient and beautiful development centered on a high-quality lifestyle and aviation, positioned to capitalize on Guyana's unprecedented economic growth.

Unparalleled Market Opportunity

Guyana is undergoing unprecedented economic growth, driven by its burgeoning oil and gas sector. GDP growth is currently the highest in the world and projected to average over 20% annually through 2028, creating increased demand for luxury housing and modern community developments.
