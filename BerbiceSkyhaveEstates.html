Investment Opportunity Overview

Berbice Skyhaven Estates represents a transformational investment opportunity in Guyana's booming economy. This premier 120-acre gated aviation community combines luxury residential living with a world-class airpark facility, positioned to capitalize on the world's fastest-growing economy.

$170M Total Project Value

4,000ft Paved Runway

Strategically positioned on the Courentyne Highway between New Amsterdam and the Suriname border, this development leverages Guyana's unprecedented economic growth driven by the oil and gas sector, creating an unparalleled opportunity for aviation enthusiasts and luxury living.

Unparalleled Market Opportunity

Guyana's economy is the world's fastest-growing, with GDP growth averaging 47% annually since 2022, projected to continue at 14% through 2029. This creates an unprecedented demand for luxury housing and aviation infrastructure.

🛩️ Aviation Excellence

4,000-foot paved runway with parallel taxiway designed for aircraft up to King Air turboprops. Hangar homes feature direct taxiway access with 60x60ft hangars. Complete with flight training academy, maintenance facilities, and 24/7 air traffic services.

🏡 Luxury Living

238 premium residential units from 2,000-3,712 sq ft featuring elevated construction, hurricane-resistant design, and luxury finishes. Each home includes covered parking, private outdoor spaces, and optional hangar attachment for aviation enthusiasts.

🌱 Sustainable Design

Innovative "Paddy Ponds" natural drainage system manages seasonal flooding while creating beautiful water features. Solar panel installations, wind power generation, and rainwater harvesting systems ensure energy independence and environmental responsibility.

🏆 Premium Amenities

Gated security with 24/7 monitoring, resort-style community center with swimming pool and fitness facilities. Tennis courts, children's playground, walking trails, and dedicated bicycle paths throughout the 120-acre community.

💼 Strategic Targeting

Positioned for oil & gas executives, mining industry professionals, international business leaders, and aviation enthusiasts. Located on the Courentyne Highway for easy access to New Amsterdam and Suriname border crossings.

💰 Investment Incentives

Tax holidays for foreign investors, import duty exemptions on construction materials and aircraft, government infrastructure support, and favorable land acquisition terms. USD-denominated pricing protects against currency fluctuation.
